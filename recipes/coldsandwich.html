Cold Sandwich Recipe

Description

A ham and cheese sandwich is a classic, satisfying meal that’s quick to prepare and endlessly customizable. At its simplest, it consists of slices of savory ham and creamy cheese layered between two pieces of bread. You can enjoy it cold for a quick lunch or grill it to golden perfection for a warm, melty treat. Common additions like mustard, mayonnaise, lettuce, tomato, or pickles can add extra flavor and texture.

This sandwich is perfect for busy days or casual meals and can be made with whatever bread and cheese you have on hand—white, whole wheat, sourdough, cheddar, Swiss, or even a croissant. Whether served fresh or toasted, a ham and cheese sandwich delivers a balanced bite of salty, creamy, and crisp goodness in every mouthful.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450 g) ground beef (or Italian sausage, or half of each)
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2–3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 jar (24 oz / ~700 g) pasta sauce (marinara or tomato-basil)
  • 9–12 lasagna noodles (regular or no-boil)
  • 15 oz (425 g) ricotta cheese (or cottage cheese)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg (optional, helps bind ricotta)
  • Salt, pepper, Italian seasoning to taste
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 400ºF.
  2. Brown the beef and onion.
  3. Prepare the baking dish and assemble the meat sauce.
  4. Begin layering the lasagna.
  5. Continue layering the lasagna.
  6. Bake the lasagna for 1 hour.
  7. Sprinkle with the remaining mozzarella and finish baking.
  8. Cool the lasagna for 15 minutes.
